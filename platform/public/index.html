Create a Scene Wiki

Scene Wiki accepts a publication root, runs the scene-mapping pipeline in the background, and publishes the finished Quartz-style wiki at a dedicated subdomain of scenewiki.net.

v1 supports Substack publication roots. Duplicate submissions resolve to the existing site or in-flight job.
